What is Carrying Capacity?

Carrying capacity simply defines how much the systems around you can provide without risk of depletion. We often forget that the resources given to us ought to be used carefully and with caution to avoid misuse and depletion. Whether it is water, land or even food, we need to think about the source and tomorrow.
